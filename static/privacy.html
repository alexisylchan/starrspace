Starr Space’s Privacy Policy

Starr Space is an web page providing users access to the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)'s astronomy photographs, as well as a way to transfer styles from the astronomy photographs to users' personal photographs.

This Privacy Policy describes how and when Starr Space collects, uses and shares your information when you use our Services. Starr Space receives your information through our APIs, applications and other third parties. For example, you send us information when you use Starr Space. When using any of our Services you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Irrespective of which country you reside in or supply information from, you authorize Starr Space to use your information in the United States and any other country where Starr Space operates.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at starr dot space at domainsbyproxy dot com.

Information Collection and Use

We collect and use your information below to provide our Services and to measure and improve our Services from time to time.

Share, Re-post & Settings: Our Services are primarily designed to help you browse NASA's astronomy photograph and share your NASA-inspired photographs with the world. Your photos are only used during the NASA-inspired-photo-creation, and are then immediately deleted from our server(s). We do NOT collect or share any personally-identifiable information or use it for profit. We only request access to your third-party accounts to enable you to share your photographs on the third-party services. We do NOT collect personally-identifiable information from your third-party accounts.

Links: Starr Space may keep track of how you interact with links across our Services by redirecting clicks or through other means. We do this to aggregate statistics such as the frequency of a particular link being clicked on, to further improve our Service. This information is not used for profit or shared with third-parties.

Cookies: Like many apps, we use cookies and similar technologies to collect additional application usage data and to improve our Services. Starr Space may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and traffic routing on our Services, and to customize and improve our Services. Most applications automatically accept cookies.

Google Analytics: We will also install Google Analytics to collect your behaviors in using our Services. We do NOT use this information to recommend any services to you. We do NOT share this information with any advertisers for profit.

Log Data: When you use Starr Space, we may receive information ("Log Data") such as your IP address, device information (including device and application IDs) and search terms. Starr Space uses Log Data to provide, understand, and improve our Services.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not disclose your private personal information except in the limited circumstances described here.

Private or Personal Information: We will NOT share or disclose your private, aggregated or otherwise personal information, such as your public user profile information, the people you share your information with, or the number of users who clicked on a particular link.
